I use AI in many of my projects with varying degrees of impact. Some projects I only use AI to help enhance certain aspects whereas in other projects I have AI build many of the core systems and I just tweak and direct it as I see fit.

One example of how I use AI is this website. I initially made the whole thing myself using HTML and CSS. Then I passed it through AI for some improvements particularly on the design side. Finally I touched up the AI's work to bring it closer to my vision. Above is a switch that you can use to see how the site was before vs after AI. Feel free to navigate to other pages and see them before AI aswell.

I see AI as a powerful tool for amplifying creativity best used when you understand what you're building well enough to direct it precisely.
